Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite food here is the recipe for delisiously rich scrambled eggs

Ingrediants

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. 1.Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. 2.Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. 3.Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. 4.Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making a omellete)
  5. 5.When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will countinue to cook on the plate until it served)
